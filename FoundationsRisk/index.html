The Situation

A benefits administrator manages 45 contributing employers, thousands of members, pension and health benefits, and strict compliance requirements — every month, manually.

Where does the biggest operational risk come from?

✓ Real risk — and you're right
Manual work is where errors enter the system.
When contributions are reconciled manually across 45 employers, one missed update — a salary change, a late termination — cascades. Invoices go out wrong. Carriers receive incorrect eligibility. Audits become painful.

But it's not the only risk. Manual errors create the data problem. And the data problem creates the visibility problem.
All three risks are connected — and they're all symptoms of the same underlying problem: fragmented administration with no single system holding the truth. That's what Oliver was built to fix.
