PPA
Platform-Powered Administration. Oliver's term for administration services delivered through modern technology. A deliberate reframe of the traditional TPA concept.
PPA signals that OLLIE is not a legacy administrator. Same work — very different operating model behind it.

A company hires an external organisation to handle enrolments, invoicing and carrier files on their behalf. That organisation is not the insurer and not the regulator — it sits between employer and carrier.

TPA
Third-party administrator. Traditional industry term. OLLIE is positioned as a modern PPA alternative.
TPAs manage the administrative layer between employers and carriers. OLLIE plays this role — but with modern technology replacing the manual model.

Back-office model
OLLIE handles operational and financial work behind the scenes. The client retains the front-end relationship with members, employers, advisors or plan sponsors.
The client is the face. OLLIE is the engine. Most brokers and advisors start here — they want the work gone, not the relationship.

Hybrid model
A tailored blend of back-office and full-service support — many clients fall between the two models.
Flexibility is core to OLLIE's offer. The level of support is a conversation, not a fixed menu — and most real clients need something in between.

System of Record
The authoritative source of truth — the single place where eligibility, billing and remittance data is official.
When OLLIE records a change, that is the answer auditors accept. Already in the system, timestamped and traceable — not reconstructed from emails.
